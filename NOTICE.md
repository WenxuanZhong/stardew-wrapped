## Stardew Valley

`Stardew Valley`, related names, marks, images, audio, and game assets belong to ConcernedApe LLC and/or their respective rights holders. They are not licensed under this repository's MIT license.

The intended full visual build is `dist-full/`, generated by `node .tools/build-full.mjs`. It includes `assets/stardew`, `assets/audio`, and `portraits` so the deployed site keeps the Stardew-style resource elements.

The optional `dist-public/` bundle generated by `node .tools/build-public.mjs` excludes copied Stardew Valley image/audio/portrait assets and uses CSS/emoji fallback visuals. Use it only when you need a rights-conservative fallback.

## Bundled Libraries

The files in `assets/vendor` are third-party libraries bundled for offline/static-site use. Preserve their upstream license notices when updating them.
